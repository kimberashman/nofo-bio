About NOFO.bio

NOFO = Notice of Funding Opportunity. The federal term NIH, NSF, ARPA-H, and BARDA use. NOFO.bio is a curated list of those, plus related programs — SBIRs, fellowships, accelerators, prizes, credit programs — for healthtech founders and biomedical PIs.

How this list is maintained

  • AI-assisted research, reviewed by a human before each entry lands.
  • Every entry links to the funder's source page. That's the authoritative record — verify there before applying.
  • Last updated in the header bumps after every verification pass. That's the freshness signal.

In scope

  • Medical, healthcare, digital health, AI in health.
  • US, UK, and EU primary funders. Selected Canada, Australia, Japan, and Israel programs. Global accelerators.
  • Specific calls with hard deadlines, and standing programs — rolling, annual, residencies.

Out of scope

  • Foundation grants for nonprofits — Instrumentl and Candid cover that better.
  • Anything I can't verify against a funder's source page.
